Tzu Chi University

What is TCU?

Tzu Chi is a private Buddhist university in Hualien City, Taiwan, that emphasizes humanitarian and humanistic values in its curriculum. In 2024, TCU merged with Tzu Chi University of Science and Technology.

Emphasis on humanistic education

The university's motto is "Kindness, Compassion, Joy, and Unselfish Giving". Students are required to take humanities courses and participate in service-learning and volunteer activities.
